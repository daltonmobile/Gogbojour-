About Gogbojour

An empath of the ancestral realm

Mmabatho was called to her gift long before she had the words for it. Today she works as an ancestral empath — reading energy, interpreting signs and symbols through ditaola/intlolo (the throwing of the bones), and translating the vibrational frequency of ko badimong for those who come to her seeking answers.

"S'khulekile ekhaya" — we greet you, from home.

Her readings take place in her Ndumba, the sacred ancestral house where the bones are cast and the ancestors speak, or telephonically for those who cannot reach her in person. Featured as Gogo Emsakazweni since October 2021, she has become a familiar voice of comfort and clairvoyance to listeners across the country.

◆ A Royal Ndebele Undertaking ◆

The Danalton Ancestral Retreat

In association with Princess Mahlangu of the Ndebele royal house, Gogbojour is opening an ancestral retreat at Danalton, eNtwana, near Siyabuswa — a home for healing, ceremony and return to self.

Where

Danalton, eNtwana, near Siyabuswa — seat of the Ndzundza-Mabhoko Ndebele royal house, a region long known for keeping Ndebele heritage, beadwork and tradition alive across generations.

What to Expect

Guided ancestral ceremony, healing consultations, and stays designed around the rhythm of traditional practice — details to be revealed as the retreat nears completion.
